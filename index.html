Our Story.

We are an IT Services company using the power of technology to build better futures for our customers, colleagues, environment and communities. Our business was created to meet the need for simple, affordable, fast and secure Managed IT Services. We believe in customization that caters to your business needs. We strive to understand the challenges that our clients are facing today and use our strong business acumen to find exclusive and unprecedented solutions for them. Our mission is to provide quality, innovative solutions that set our clients apart. The art of polarization is more than just taking a hard position on a subject. It is about understanding what makes you unique, and how to turn that into an asset. Digital Polaroid provides technological solutions dedicated to doing exactly that. By taking the time to understand who you are and what you stand for, we strive to provide the best quality end-to-end solutions from strategy to deployment and beyond.

Digital Polaroid Technologies was founded because we were tired of watching companies, business owners and IT Managers struggle with ineffective and dated IT solutions, exorbitant prices and constant downtime. For this reason, we have specialized in providing outsourced technology solutions to help fulfil the increasing need for outsourced advisory & consulting services in Southern Africa and abroad. Digital Polaroid has a clientele of complex projects ranging from highly customized web applications to social media management. Every solution we provide is handpicked to ensure long-term competitive edge around a shrewd policy agenda. Our core competency is the design and development of web-based solutions, and branding. We have a proven track record to meet inhuman deadlines and our motto is to deliver quality work at a lightning pace without exceeding the budget. We believe our work speaks for itself, and invite you to see some of our past work for a glimpse of what we can do for you and your business.

More About Us.

Papias Banda – Managing Director Papias is passionate about technology and community. The evolution of different technologies and new advancements continue to drive innovation within the company. His commitment to delivering integrated solutions has led to him engaging with executives, business owners, IT professionals and entrepreneurs alike to understand their IT challenges and to provide them with various technological solutions that tick all the boxes, and then some. Papias began his career as an IT Intern in the medical industry, and his approach to creating optimized solutions naturally led him into a consulting role. Under Papias' leadership, Digital Polaroid reflects the same qualities and enthusiasm towards technology. Our services are based on the seven core values of Quality, Simplicity, Integrity, Trust, Responsibility, Sharing and Passion. to become the go to Managed Technology Services provider for companies who are searching for customer centric service, cost-effective solutions and industry leading technology. When Papias is offline, he can be found chasing his love for new and adventurous experiences, or spending quality time with his family.
